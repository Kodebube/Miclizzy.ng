About Us

Miclizzy Distribution Limited is a pharmautical company, which is based in Nigeria. We deliver and distribute injections and surgical equipments to hospitals, clinics and health centers in Nigeria and even some parts of West-Africa.

Our goal is to provide the public with good drugs (which we've gotten from licensed drug manufacturing companies). Your health is our priority, so we've taken it upon our selves to filter out the best of the best drugs, which is certain to accomplish its purpose in your body (or the body of the patient).

Our products are licensed and certified by National Agency for food, drug and administration control (NAFDAC) and the standard organization of Nigeria (SON) respectively.
